About Accomplit

Accomplit is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help students organize their achievements, experiences, and academic milestones in one secure place. Users can store awards, projects, volunteer work, certifications, and personal accomplishments as they grow. With this information, Accomplit instantly generates professional resumes, cover letters, and other career documents tailored to school, job, or scholarship applications.

Created with students in mind, the app makes it easy to stay organized, confident, and ready for any opportunity. Whether you're applying for your first internship or building a college portfolio, Accomplit helps you present your best self.

Meet the Creators

Audrey & Maggie

Accomplit was created by two 15-year-old students who saw how difficult it was for their classmates to keep track of achievements over the years. They realized that most students only start preparing resumes at the last minute, often forgetting important activities and experiences. Motivated to make career readiness easier and less stressful, they combined their love for technology and design to build Accomplit. Their goal is to help young people stay organized, confident, and ready for future opportunities long before deadlines arrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower every student to recognize their potential by giving them the tools to track achievements, build strong career documents, and approach their future with confidence and preparation.
